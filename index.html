Welcome To KPS

Prof. K.A Adeyemo Proprietor

Kasmoz Private School (KPS), is a prestigious Nigerian private secondary school dedicated to providing an exceptional educational experience for its students. Established in 2010, KPS has been at the forefront of academic excellence and character development, nurturing young minds to reach their full potential.

Located in the vibrant city of Ibadan, KPS boasts a sprawling campus equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and a stimulating learning environment. Our school prides itself on its commitment to holistic education, encompassing academics, sports, arts, and character-building activities.

Academics at KPS are centered around a rigorous curriculum that follows the Nigerian national standards, augmented with global perspectives to prepare our students for a competitive world. Our dedicated team of highly qualified teachers utilizes innovative teaching methods, technology integration, and personalized instruction to cater to the diverse learning styles of our students. Our School

Crèche

With a focus on play-based learning, our curriculum sparks creativity, curiosity, and exploration. Through age-appropriate activities, your child will be engaged in storytelling, arts and crafts, music, and interactive games, fostering a love for learning from an early age. You can be rest assured that your child will be in the best hands, receiving individualized attention and care.

Nursery & Primary School

In our nursery, we lay the foundation for early literacy, numeracy, and social skills, fostering a warm and secure environment for your child's growth. As they transition to our primary school, they will build upon their knowledge, expand their horizons, and explore a wide range of subjects, including language arts, mathematics, sciences, arts, and physical education.
